Riadhatul A'mal School App UI Design

Developed a comprehensive school management application for Riadhatul A'mal, focusing on enhancing communication between teachers, students, and parents. The app includes features for teachers to upload homework and materials notifications, provide feedback, and offer extra exercises for academically weak students. Students and parents have their interfaces to access relevant information, with parents also able to monitor their children's progress and results.
