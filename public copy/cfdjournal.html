CFD for Efficient Journal Publication

I worked on a project as part of the team to improve the CFD system proprietary software that supports most journal publication processes publicly released. As part of my role, I worked on improving the journal review process and simplifying submission & upload functionality. This will make possible complex interactions that can optimize the user registration and submission mechanism, improve the data use efficiency of users, provide a notification system, and help check for plagiarism. We also provided new ways to help journals staff, and everyone from Journal Managers, Editors and Copyeditors.
